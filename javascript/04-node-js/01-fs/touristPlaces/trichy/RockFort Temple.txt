The Rockfort Temple, also called Ucchi Pillaiyar Kovil, is Tiruchirappalli's famed landmark, standing 275 ft tall at the city's core. Accessible via a staircase of 437 steps, it houses the Sri Thayumanaswamy Temple halfway up, featuring a 100-pillared hall and Vimana dating back to the 3rd century B.C. From its summit, visitors enjoy breathtaking views of Srirangam, the Cauvery River, and other landmarks.
